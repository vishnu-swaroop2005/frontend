Career Counseling Test

1. When working on a project, how do you prefer to approach it?

2. How do you feel about making decisions?

3. How do you prefer to communicate with others?

4. In a group setting, what role do you naturally take on?

5. When you face a new challenge, how do you usually feel?

6. When dealing with a problem, how do you solve it?

7. How do you handle change or unpredictability in life?

8. How do you typically make important life decisions?

9. What type of work environment do you prefer?

10. When you have to make a decision under pressure, how do you act?

11. How do you handle multiple tasks or deadlines?

12. What motivates you most in your work or studies?

13. When working on a team project, what’s most important to you?

14. When faced with an unexpected obstacle, how do you react?

15. What type of task excites you the most?
